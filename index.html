XR Campus Tour

An interactive journey bridging reality and digital storytelling through location-based AR technology.

Explore Process

About This Portfolio

This website documents the full development process of the XR Campus Tour project. Instead of only presenting the final outcome, this portfolio focuses on the design process, research, experimentation, and iterative development behind the project.

Project Overview

The project aims to enhance traditional campus visits by introducing interactive digital content at specific physical locations. When users approach a landmark, an AR character appears and introduces the location through narration.

Key Concepts

📍

Location-Based Interaction

The application detects the user's geographic position and triggers interactive XR content when the user approaches predefined campus landmarks.

🤖

AR Guide

A virtual guide character appears to introduce campus locations.

✨

Immersive Exploration

Transforming a simple walk into an engaging story-driven tour.

Design Process

01

Initial Concept

The project began with the idea of improving the campus tour experience for visitors and new students. Traditional tours often rely on static maps or human guides, which may not always be available.

02

Research

Research focused on extended reality technologies, location-based interaction systems, and existing digital tour applications. The goal was to identify how XR could enhance spatial storytelling.

03

Interaction Design

Different interaction models were explored, including how users trigger content, how the AR guide appears, and how information should be presented to maintain engagement without overwhelming users.

04

Prototype Development

A prototype was developed using XR development tools. The system implemented location detection, AR visualization, and a basic virtual guide character that introduces selected landmarks.

05

Testing and Refinement

The prototype was evaluated and refined by adjusting trigger distances, improving guide narration, and optimizing interaction flow to make the experience smoother for users.
