About This Portfolio

This website documents the full development process of the XR Campus Tour project. Instead of only presenting the final outcome, this portfolio focuses on the design process, research, and iterative development.

Project Overview

The project aims to enhance traditional campus visits by introducing interactive digital content through AR characters that appear at specific physical locations.

Key Concepts

📍

Location-Based Interaction

Using GPS to trigger immersive digital overlays exactly where they matter most.

🤖

AR Guide

An intelligent virtual companion for campus exploration.

Immersive Storytelling

Turning a simple campus walk into a rich, interactive historical adventure.
